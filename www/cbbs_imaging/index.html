How Do You Neuroimaging?

Take the Survey

The goal of the CBBS imaging platform is to develop and provide a shared computing infrastructure for brain imaging research in Magdeburg. This platform aims to streamline routine data preparation and processing tasks, and to offer cutting edge data analysis techniques, developed here in Magdeburg or elsewhere.

The results of this survey will inform the development of the imaging platform and are the basis for a prioritization of work packages.

Please help us to assemble an accurate picture on established practices and tools used and developed in Magdeburg. Ideally, each lab working with brain imaging will have this survey completed by one representative who has a good understanding of routines and technical infrastructure used in the lab.

For any inquiries, please contact Michael Hanke.
